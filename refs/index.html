Referral Links

Amazon

Buy stuff, Referred gets $0, Referring gets a small percentage. No idea if it works since I've stopped hosting anything.
Republic Wireless

A new kind of cell phone plan - talk over your WiFi when you can, with Sprint network as a backup, plans from $10 unlimited talk text. Their referral program is recently back after a re-do. Referred and referring each get $20 off a future bill.

Bing Rewards

Switch to bing for your web searches, and you can get about $5/month in Amazon credit, or some other popular retailers. Referred gets 200 pts, Referring gets 200 pts (~$2 in rewards). If you use Hulu Plus, it's possible to earn enough points every month to keep a free subscription.
Trello

Organize lists / cards / projects. I use it for work and personal "todo" lists. Referring gets 1 mo. free "Gold" status - someting about stickers, I don't really care, but I like the site and the Android app a lot.

G2 Reviews

For IT tool reviews - log in with LinkedIn and write a review of any software you use for work. Referred and Referrer both get $5 Amazon gift card.
